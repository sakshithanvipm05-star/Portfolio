Featured Side Project
⚡ Flagship Project

AI PRD Generator

A product manager's superpower — an AI tool that transforms rough product ideas into structured, stakeholder-ready Product Requirement Documents in minutes, not days.

Problem it solves

Writing PRDs is one of the most time-consuming tasks in a PM's workflow. It often takes 2–5 days of drafting, reviewing, and aligning stakeholders. Junior PMs especially struggle with structure, completeness, and the right level of detail.

Target users

  • Product Managers at early-stage startups
  • Junior PMs learning to write structured docs
  • Founders doing product discovery solo
  • Consultants needing rapid documentation

Why it's impactful

Reduces PRD creation from days to minutes. Ensures completeness by prompting for all key sections. Creates a consistent format across teams, and lowers the barrier for non-technical stakeholders to contribute to product thinking.

Future roadmap

  • JIRA / Linear integration for auto-ticket creation
  • Competitor analysis injection via web search
  • Team collaboration and commenting layer
  • Version history and PRD changelog tracking

How it works — step by step

STEP 01
Input product idea or problem statement
STEP 02
AI prompts for context: users, goals, constraints
STEP 03
Generates structured PRD with all sections
STEP 04
PM reviews, edits, and exports or shares
STEP 05
Stakeholders align faster, sprint begins
